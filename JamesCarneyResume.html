Mail Reaper 9000
Developed a tool to periodically automatically archive and label emails in a Gmail inbox per a user defined schedule, and generate a digest report of successful transactions per executions. Main script is compiled from TypeScript with Webpack, executes in the Google Apps Script runtime, and deploys with clasp.
